High-Performance 3D Printer

This printer was built from scratch using mostly 3D printed parts and open-source hardware. It’s tuned for high-speed, high-temperature printing and is the workhorse behind the plane, cube, and a lot of client parts.

Hardware & Capabilities

  • CoreXY layout with rigid frame and direct-drive toolhead for clean motion at high accelerations.
  • All-metal hotend capable of 500 °C and enclosed build volume for engineering plastics.
  • Custom wiring and cable management to keep the motion system light and serviceable.

Firmware & Calibration

  • Klipper-based firmware with pressure advance, input shaping, and tuned acceleration profiles.
  • Custom macros for material swaps, nozzle cleaning, and repeatable calibration routines.
  • Dialed in profiles for PLA, PETG, ASA, Nylon, and flexible materials.
