Custom Light Display

This cube is a fully custom light sculpture built around ~1000 individually-addressable LEDs, double-sided mirrors, and a 3D printed structure that hides wiring while still letting the light breathe.

Design & Hardware

  • SolidWorks for parametric frame design, snap-fit panels, and mirror recesses.
  • 3D printed PLA structure tuned for even light diffusion and clean cable routing.
  • Dense LED matrix layout with respect to thermals, accessibility, and serviceability.

Software & Animations

  • Custom animation engine to generate 3D patterns, waves, and audio-reactive effects.
  • Configurable scenes: ambient modes, “showcase” patterns, and low-brightness night presets.
  • Optimized update loop to keep transitions smooth while staying within power budget.
