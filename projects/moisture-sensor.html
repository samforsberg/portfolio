IoT Moisture Sensor Node

A low-power sensor node designed to live in walls and crawlspaces, watching for moisture before it becomes damage. Built around reliable readings, long battery life, and a simple deployment story.

Role Hardware design, firmware, enclosure
Tools KiCad · ESP32 · LoRa · custom PCB
Focus Reliability · power · manufacturability

Overview

The moisture sensor node is part of a larger system aimed at catching leaks early. The board pairs a capacitance-based moisture sensor with an ESP32 and LoRa radio, so deployed units can quietly report environmental data over long ranges without Wi-Fi.

Design details

  • Custom PCB with moisture sensing front-end and radio
  • Firmware focused on deep-sleep and low average current draw
  • Mechanical design for easy mounting in real buildings

Why it matters

Water damage is expensive and usually discovered late. By making these nodes cheap, small, and simple to install, you can turn passive buildings into systems that surface problems before they become disasters.
