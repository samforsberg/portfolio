In-Wall Moisture Sensors

This system uses low-power, low-frequency wireless nodes to quietly monitor moisture inside walls over long periods. The goal: catch leaks early, before they turn into mold, rot, and expensive tear-outs.

Electronics & RF

  • Custom PCBs with low-level microcontrollers, sensor front-ends, and RF sections.
  • Sub-GHz communication for better penetration through walls and longer range.
  • Careful antenna layout and tuning to balance performance with small enclosure size.

Mechanical & Enclosure Design

  • ASA housings designed to survive humid, hot, and dusty environments.
  • Mounting geometry to sit behind drywall while staying serviceable if needed.
  • Thought about install workflow for contractors: drill, insert, pair, and walk away.
